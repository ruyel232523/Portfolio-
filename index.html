About Me

A geotechnical engineer who believes every great structure starts beneath the surface.

I'm Miah Md. Ruyel, currently pursuing my Master's degree in Geotechnical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, one of Europe's leading technical universities. With over 4 years of professional experience spanning both governmental and private sector projects, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical expertise to every challenge.

My work revolves around understanding the ground beneath us — from soil investigation and foundation design to slope stability analysis and ground improvement techniques. I'm passionate about applying innovative solutions to complex geotechnical problems and ensuring the safety and longevity of critical infrastructure.

Whether it's designing foundations for high-rise buildings, analyzing soil behavior under seismic loads, or conducting comprehensive site investigations, I approach every project with precision, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to engineering excellence.
